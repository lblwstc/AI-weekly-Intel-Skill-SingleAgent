# Research Rules

Use primary sources first. For OpenAI-specific requests, start with:

- `openai.com/index/...`
- `help.openai.com/...`
- official docs or changelogs when relevant

Use tech media only when:

- confirming broader market framing
- resolving ambiguity between official snippets
- the user explicitly asks for outside coverage

Rules:

- Anchor the reporting window with exact dates.
- Save a source manifest before synthesizing.
- Do not rely on memory for "latest" updates.
- Prefer 5 to 10 items instead of bloating the brief.
- Normalize titles and dates consistently before writing the memo.

Recommended synthesis lens:

- What materially changed for enterprise workflows?
- What moved from experiment to deployable workflow?
- What governance or safety evidence shipped with the capability?
- Which functions inside a company benefit first?

Good business categories:

- Productivity
- Coding and security
- Finance and spreadsheets
- Research and technical discovery
- Governance and rollout readiness
